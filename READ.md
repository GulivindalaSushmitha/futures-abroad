# Futures Abroad - AI-Powered University Journey Platform

## 🎓 Overview

Futures Abroad is an AI-assisted web platform for high school students (Grade 10+) that guides them from self-discovery to competitive university applications. The platform acts as a persistent, personalized counselor — surfacing relevant activities, guiding registrations, tracking progress, and routing students into the Futures Abroad admissions program.

## ✨ Features

- **AI-Powered Interest Discovery**: Conversational quiz that creates personalized interest profiles
- **Smart Activity Matching**: Curated recommendations for extracurriculars, internships, and programs
- **Registration Guidance**: Step-by-step checklists with deadline reminders
- **Portfolio Building**: Track activities, reflections, and skill development
- **University Shortlisting**: Personalized university recommendations with reach/target/safety breakdown
- **Counselor Handoff**: Seamless transition to expert guidance
