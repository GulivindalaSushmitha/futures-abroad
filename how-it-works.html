How It Works

Your 6-Step Journey to
University Success

Every phase is powered by AI and personalized to your unique profile

01

Onboarding & Interest Discovery

📋 What happens?

The student creates an account and is immediately guided through an AI-driven interest discovery quiz. The output is a named interest profile that drives all future recommendations.

📝 Step-by-Step

  • Student lands on sign-up page — enters name, email, grade (10/11/12), school name, and country
  • App creates a basic profile and redirects to the AI Interest Quiz
  • AI conducts a 5-8 question conversational quiz (chat-style UI, not a form)
  • AI generates an Interest Profile: a set of 2-4 tags (e.g., STEM + Leadership + Sustainability)
  • Student can edit or confirm the profile

🤖 AI Behavior

  • Conversational chat interface — NOT a multiple-choice form
  • Adaptive branching based on answers
  • Limit to 8 questions maximum
  • Infer profile from partial answers if student exits early

📊 Inputs & Outputs

  • Inputs: Name, grade, school, country, quiz responses
  • Outputs: Student profile object with interest tags

02

Curated Activity Recommendations

📋 What happens?

Based on the interest profile, the AI surfaces a personalised feed of 5-8 activities. Each activity card shows what it is, why it was recommended, and what the student will gain.

📝 Step-by-Step

  • On profile confirmation, AI queries the activity database filtered by interest tags, grade, country, and cost
  • Top 5-8 matches are presented as activity cards in a feed view
  • Each card shows: Activity name, type, duration, cost, deadline, and a 1-line AI rationale
  • Student can filter by type, duration, cost, and location
  • Student can tap any card to open a detail view with AI Q&A chat

🤖 Recommendation Engine

  • Primary sort: Interest tag match score (weighted)
  • Secondary sort: Deadline proximity and grade-appropriateness
  • Deprioritise activities the student has already completed or saved
  • Refresh feed weekly with new activities; notify student of new matches
  • If fewer than 5 matches: broaden tag matching to adjacent interest areas

📊 Activity Data Model

  • id, name, type (internship/competition/volunteering/course/workshop/research)
  • interest_tags, grade_min/grade_max, country, cost
  • duration, deadline, skills_gained, registration_url

Find Activities
03

Activity Selection & Registration

📋 What happens?

When a student selects an activity, the AI walks them through registration step by step — preparing materials, tracking deadlines, and sending reminders.

📝 Step-by-Step

  • Student taps 'Register' on an activity card
  • AI confirms selection and shows a personalised readiness summary
  • AI generates a registration checklist tailored to the activity
  • Checklist items are actionable tasks with sub-steps
  • Deadline is added to the in-app calendar with reminders
  • Student marks the activity as 'Registered' — status updates in their portfolio

📋 Checklist Logic

  • Items are generated dynamically from registration_requirements field
  • Common items: personal statement draft, teacher recommendation, parent permission, fee payment
  • AI provides tips per item — e.g., for 'personal statement': 'Keep it under 250 words'
  • Items can be assigned due dates independently of the main deadline

🔔 Reminders

  • Push notifications and email reminders at: 2 weeks, 1 week, 3 days, and 1 day before deadline
Phase 3
Start Registering
04

Post-Activity Guidance

📋 What happens?

After an activity is completed, the AI helps the student reflect, log the experience to their portfolio, and surface the next step in their journey.

📝 Step-by-Step

  • Student marks activity as 'Completed'
  • App prompts for completion date and a brief outcome note
  • AI adds the activity to the student's Portfolio
  • AI presents a Reflection Prompt — 2-3 guided questions
  • Student's reflection responses are saved as private notes
  • AI updates the student's profile strength score
  • AI surfaces the next recommended activity or milestone

📊 Portfolio Object

  • activity_id, date_completed, duration, skills[], student_note
  • reflection_responses[], essay_potential_flag
  • Portfolio is exportable as PDF for counselor handoff

📈 Profile Strength Score (0-100)

  • Number of completed activities (max 5 counted) — 30 pts
  • Diversity of activity types — 20 pts
  • Evidence of leadership or initiative — 20 pts
  • Recency (activity completed within last 12 months) — 15 pts
  • Reflection quality (length and depth) — 15 pts
Phase 4
Build Portfolio
05

Grade-Based University Pathway

⭐ Critical

📋 What happens?

The app detects the student's grade and forks the experience. Grade 11 students receive a university shortlist and a prominent Futures Abroad CTA.

🎯 PRIMARY CONVERSION POINT: This is the primary conversion point for Futures Abroad enrollment. The UX here must be high-quality and personalised.

🎓 Grade 11 Experience

  • App detects Grade 11 status and shows: "You're starting Grade 11 — let's get serious about universities"
  • AI generates a preliminary university shortlist (5-8 universities)
  • Each university card shows: name, country, notable programs, acceptance rate range, AI rationale
  • Shortlist is editable — student can add/remove universities
  • Futures Abroad CTA: "Ready to turn this shortlist into a real application? Book your first session with a Futures Abroad counselor."

🌱 Grade 10 Experience

  • Profile progress bar: "You are X% toward a competitive Grade 11 profile"
  • Gap analysis: AI identifies missing activity types
  • Roadmap view: shows recommended milestones month by month through Grade 11
  • Soft CTA: "Want expert guidance from Day 1? Learn about Futures Abroad."

🏛️ University Shortlist Logic

  • Pull from a curated university database
  • Match on: student interest tags → program offerings, target country, self-reported GPA band
  • Include a mix: 2 reach, 3 target, 2 safety (labelled clearly)
Phase 5
Book Your University Planning Session
06

Futures Abroad Handoff

📋 What happens?

When a student accepts the Futures Abroad CTA, the app exports their profile and connects them directly with a counselor. This phase completes the app's core funnel.

📝 Step-by-Step

  • Student taps 'Register with Futures Abroad' CTA
  • App auto-generates a Profile Summary PDF
  • Student reviews and confirms the PDF before it is shared
  • Student is shown Futures Abroad program options (Grade 10-12 track, Grade 11 fast-track) with pricing
  • Student completes a short intake form (pre-filled from profile)
  • Counselor is notified with the student's profile PDF
  • A first session is scheduled via in-app calendar (Calendly integration)
  • Student enters the Futures Abroad full program

📄 Profile Summary PDF Contents

  • Student name, grade, school, country
  • Interest profile tags + AI-generated summary paragraph
  • Completed activities list with dates and skills
  • Reflection highlights flagged as essay-potential
  • Preliminary university shortlist
  • Profile strength score and gap analysis

🔄 Post-Enrollment App Behaviour

  • Counselor can push tasks (essay drafts, test prep schedules) into the student's app
  • Student's activity feed continues — FA counselor can add recommended activities directly
  • Parent dashboard remains active with milestone notifications
